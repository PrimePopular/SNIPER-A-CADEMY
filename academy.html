Course overview

Everything is sequenced. Nothing is optional.

Four modules, built in the order the market actually teaches them — from foundational structure to the discipline that keeps you in the game after a losing week.

01

Beginner Roadmap

Market structure, order types, and the vocabulary every later module assumes you already know. This is the floor everything else is built on.

02

Intermediate Strategies

Entry frameworks and confluence — how to combine liquidity, structure, and timing into setups worth actually risking capital on.

03

Trading Psychology

Tracking your own behavior around wins, losses, and revenge trades — because the leak is usually in the trader, not the strategy.

04

Risk Management

Position sizing, drawdown limits, and the rules that keep one bad week from becoming a blown account.
